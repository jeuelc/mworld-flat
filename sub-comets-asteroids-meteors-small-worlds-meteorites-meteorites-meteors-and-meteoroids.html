Meteorites, meteors and meteoroids

Meteorite, meteor or meteoroid? It sounds confusing! The words are almost the same, so what are they and how are they different from each other?

Meteoroids are small objects floating in space, made of rock or metal.

Meteors are meteoroids and asteroids that are falling through the Earth's atmosphere. These are what we see when we see a 'shooting star' at night!

Meteorites are pieces of meteors that reach the surface of the Earth.
