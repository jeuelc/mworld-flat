Bottoms up!

The deepest point across all the oceans is in the Mariana Trench in the Pacific Ocean and its 10,944 metres deep! That's the same as an upside down Mt Everest plus a bit more!

Imagine getting into a submarine and travelling down down down you're now at 150 metres deep and there is no more light; you've entered the twilight zone. There is so much pressure down here that the only safe place for us humans would be in a submarine.

When your submarine gets to 1000 metres, you have reached the dark zone come and explore the marine animals of the deep!
