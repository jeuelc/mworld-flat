Eat Your Own Poo

Small herbivores such as rabbits eat grass and plants which can be easy to find, but don't have much protein in them for building muscles. However, the bacteria in their intestines are very good at making protein from the parts of plants the rabbit can't digest - but the rabbit can't digest the bacteria in their intestines.

So rabbits have found a way to get that protein: at night, the rabbit produces a special poo that has lots of bacteria in it. The rabbit eats that special poo and gets the extra protein that it needs. While this is great for the rabbit, it is not good for people because our intestines are not like a rabbit's!
