Spear Time!

If you need to catch some dinner, chasing it down can be hard work - there must be a better way! Some animals use nifty tricks to catch their food. Snails called cone shells make little harpoons that they can fire at an unsuspecting fish, pulling it back into their mouths using a long thread. The harpoon is also highly poisonous. It's not only able to kill small fish but some can kill people!

Jellyfish floating in the sea can look completely harmless, but they also make mini-spears that they fire from very long tentacles when an animal touches them. Box jellyfish are particularly dangerous, so be careful not to touch them even when they're on the beach!
