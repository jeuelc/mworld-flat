Who Eats Who?

The first thing you need to know when talking about animals and eating is - what types of food are there? Animals that eat plants are called herbivores, while carnivores are meat-eaters. But even within these food groups, there are many different types of foods. Some of these are easy to get and some aren't.

Other animals like a mix of foods - omnivores eat a wide selection of foods including plants and meat. There are also many animals that eat dirt or tiny food floating in water - is there really enough food there? How do they really get their food? And can they eat anything?
