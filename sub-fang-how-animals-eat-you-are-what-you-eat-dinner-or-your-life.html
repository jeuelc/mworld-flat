Dinner or your life

 
click here click here

How hungry are you? Imagine you're a lion and haven't eaten for days, and you've got to chase that antelope to eat it. The antelope runs really fast, jumping over rocks and bushes. While you're chasing the antelope, you slip and nearly break your leg. Now, you could jump up and keep chasing, but you may really hurt yourself is it worth it? Hopefully you'll find another antelope later - it's only your dinner you're chasing.

Now imagine that you're that antelope, the lion is hot on your heels, and if he catches you, you'll be his dinner! If you slip and he doesn't get you, you'd better get up and keep going, because you're running for your life!

Animals running to save themselves will probably try harder than animals just chasing their dinner.
