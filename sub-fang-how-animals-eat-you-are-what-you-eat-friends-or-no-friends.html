Do some foods give you bad breath? Maybe that would mean no friends. But the food you eat can influence how many friends you would hang around with.

Imagine we take two herbivores, a browser that eats leaves and a grazer that eats grass. The leaf-eater needs to find trees for its food, but these can often be hard to find or reach. And when you do find them, you want to make sure that you get as much as you can without sharing with anyone. So browsers often live by themselves so they don't have to share food with others.?

If you eat grass though, there can be heaps for everyone in big grasslands, so it's fine to have lots of friends with you. Predators can find you more easily in grasslands, so it actually helps to have friends around that can help spot the lion lurking in the grass! Grazers are often found in herds, like cows.
