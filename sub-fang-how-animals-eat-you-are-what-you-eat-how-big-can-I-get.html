Did you know that the biggest animals that live on land eat plants! These include elephants, hippos and rhinos. Why don't we have giant carnivorous lions as big as elephants? This is because when you're big you need to eat a huge amount of food.

In the African savannah there are great expanses of grasses and trees, enough to feed a herd of elephants. But there's nowhere near enough meat to feed a giant lion.

Whales have done something tricky - they've found a massive food source in the ocean to keep them going. This food source is tiny krill and there are so many of these tiny animals that they can feed the largest animals ever to exist!
