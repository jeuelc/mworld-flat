Fang ... How Animals Eat!

Animals are fuelled by food they must eat to get enough nutrients and energy to survive.

The world is like a smorgasbord for animals so many different foods to eat! But how to choose what to eat? And how to chew it up and digest it?

In this story, we'll learn about many of the foods of animals (spit stew anyone?), how they eat them (can you push your stomach out of your mouth?) and what it means about how big they can get.
