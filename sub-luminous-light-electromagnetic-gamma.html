Gamma Rays

 
click here

Gamma rays are one of the most high-energy types of invisible light. A gamma ray has more energy than other forms of visible or invisible light. Gamma rays are made by certain unstable atoms. These atoms are called radioactive, and some of them send out gamma rays. Gamma rays also come to us from outer space! There are special gamma ray telescopes that can take images of outer space using gamma rays!

High energy gamma rays from space can also make a kind of avalanche. Instead of this avalanche being made from snow, a gamma-ray avalanche is caused when a gamma ray from space hits particles of air very high in the sky. This can break the particles of air into pieces. These pieces fly off at very high speed and can break up even more air particles. Soon you have an avalanche of broken air particles, and other particles, caused by the gamma ray from space!
