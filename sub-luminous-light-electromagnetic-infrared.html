Infrared (IR) light is another form of light we cannot see. It is a "heat ray" form of light. If you are standing in a completely dark room, next to a metal radiator, you can feel the heat rays even though your eyes cannot see anything. These heat rays are "'infrared light"'!

Even though our eyes cannot see infrared light, we can wear special goggles that will let us see warm things at night! Since people are warm, these special goggles will let you see other people even if it is completely dark! Some of these "infrared pictures" are shown below. All warm bodies shine infrared light.
