Microwaves

 

When you put food in a microwave oven, it shines invisible microwave light on your food, which heats it up! There are lots of other uses for microwaves. Microwaves are used to send information to and from the satellites which orbit the Earth.

Also, lots of microwaves come to us from outer space! This is called the 'cosmic microwave background radiation", and it is very old light. It is like an "invisible light fossil" which tells us a lot about how the universe behaved billions and billions of years ago!

Even stranger, this "cosmic microwave background" is really really cold: about minus 270 degrees Celsius!
