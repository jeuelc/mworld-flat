Radio waves are an important type of invisible light. Information in the wireless internet is carried using radio waves! Just as an ordinary visible light beam can travel very quickly from one place to another, so too the invisible light called radio waves can travel very quickly. This means that the information detected by your mobile phone or your wireless internet gets to you fast!

Wireless internet is not the only use for the invisible light called radio waves. Did you know that radio waves come to Earth from space? Just as telescopes can be used to see objects in space using visible light, there are special telescopes called radio telescopes which can use radio waves to see things in outer space!
