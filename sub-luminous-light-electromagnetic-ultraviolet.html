Ultraviolet (UV) Light

 
click here

There is ultraviolet (UV) invisible light in sunlight. Too much UV can damage your skin, giving you sunburn when you stay in the sun for too long.

Even though ultraviolet light is invisible to us, there are some insects that can see UV light! Some flowers have "secret patterns" which are only visible to creatures such as insects which can see UV!

The patterns on computer chips, such as the ones in your mobile phone or tablet computer or desktop computer, can be made with UV light! Also, when UV light is shone on some substances, they glow by sending out visible light! This is called "fluorescence". Some clothes can glow in the dark when UV light is shone on them!
