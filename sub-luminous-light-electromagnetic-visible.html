Visible

Visible light comes in many colours. Can you name the colours of the rainbow? There are seven.

We can make other colours by mixing together these colours of light. Did you know that white is the colour of light you get when you mix all of the colours of the rainbow together? Other colours, such as pink and brown, can be obtained by mixing a few colours of light together.

Light of various colours is shining from your screen into your eyes, which is why you can read this sentence! The different colours are called visible light, because you can see them! This may seem like a silly thing to say, but as you will see, some colours of light are invisible!
