X-rays

 

X-rays are another example of invisible light.

X-rays can pass through your body like visible light can pass through glass, but they dont pass through your bones. This means that X-rays can be used to take photographs of the bones in your body! They can also be used to take pictures of other things inside your body, such as your teeth and your heart.

Have you, or anyone else you know had an X-ray image taken of some bones?

When doctors take X-ray pictures, the X-rays pass all the way through your body to the camera which takes a picture of your insides. How weird and wonderful is that!
