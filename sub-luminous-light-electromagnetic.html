Electromagnetic Spectrum

There are many types of light and they all carry different energies. We often refer to these different energies as colours. Light comes in all the colours of the rainbow; red, orange, yellow, green, blue, indigo and violet.

There are also some special colours, which we call invisible light. That's light that you can't see! Just because you can't see invisible light, that doesn't mean it is not there. In fact, invisible light is around us all the time! If this light is invisible, how do you think we know it's there?

All of these forms of light, visible and invisible together, form what we call the electromagnetic spectrum.
