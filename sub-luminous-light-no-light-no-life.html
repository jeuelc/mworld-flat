No light, no life!

Why do you think light is essential for life on Earth?

Without light, there would be no life on Earth! No trees, no butterflies, no people!

Light carries energy from the sun, which lights up the Earth so that you can see the world around you! Even more important, energy from the sun is soaked up by plants which use it to help them grow! This energy is then available to other forms of life, such as any living thing that eats the plant to get energy.

You eat plants to get energy, for example when you have a slice of bread. In your slice of bread, the sun gave the energy to the wheat plant, which is now giving energy to you as you eat the bread! So, in a sense you are eating sunlight when you eat bread!
