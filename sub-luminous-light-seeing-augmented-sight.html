What is the smallest thing that you can see? An ant? A speck of dust? Did you know that there are lots of things that are too small for us to see? These things can be much smaller than an ant or a speck of dust.

How do we know about these tiny things? We know because we can build microscopes! Microscopes are like super-powerful magnifying glasses that let us see tiny things. What kinds of tiny things? Well, let's take a drop of water from a river or a pond, and look at it using a microscope. You will see lots of weird tiny creatures swimming around, which are too small to see with your eye alone. Have a look at the pictures of some of these tiny creatures!
