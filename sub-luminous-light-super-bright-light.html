Super bright light!

We can make very bright beams of light. A laser is a good example. The red laser pointer you can buy in shops is a very bright beam of light. Some lasers that are so bright they can be used to cut steel! This is super-bright light!

Another form of super-bright light is when a star explodes. Such an explosion is called a supernova. When a star explodes in this way, it sometimes makes such a super-bright burst of light that the exploding star can been seen during the day! Another form of super-bright light is the military laser, which can be used to destroy missiles.
