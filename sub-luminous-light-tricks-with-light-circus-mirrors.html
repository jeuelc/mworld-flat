Circus mirrors (reflection)

 

The surfaces of most mirrors are flat. What happens when you look at yourself in a mirror that has a wavy surface? You will look funny! Some parts of you will look bigger than they really are, and other parts will look smaller. Some parts will even seem stretched in strange directions!

These strange mirrors work because their wavy surfaces distort how light is reflected from them. Light from the sun bounces off you, then it bounces off the mirror. When this distorted light that is bounced from the mirror reaches your eye, the resulting image looks weird!
