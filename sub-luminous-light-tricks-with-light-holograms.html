Another nice trick with light is called a 'hologram'. This is a special picture which looks three-dimensional, just like the world around you.

Some credit cards have holograms on them, which look like small metal pieces of foil that seem three-dimensional when you shine light on them. These pieces of foil have very detailed patterns scratched into them. When light bounces off the foil hologram, it is the same as the light that would have bounced off a three-dimensional object sitting behind the hologram. When this bounced-off light reaches your eye, the eye is tricked into thinking that there is a three-dimensional object behind the foil!
