Iridescence

 

Some material has a fascinating property which causes it to appear to change colour depending upon how you look at it!

This property is known as iridescence. Objects which exhibit iridescence appear to have different colours when light strikes it from different angles. You may have seen iridescence in action before! For example, soap bubbles, peacock feathers, and some sea shells all appear to have different colours in different places when they are moved around.
