What is it?

What is light? Light is a form of energy which moves through space. How do we know it carries energy? Because our faces feel warm when we stand outside on a sunny day!

Light moves very quickly. It moves so fast that, in one second, a light beam could run around the whole world seven times!

Light lets us see the world around us. Light has many colours. Most of the light on Earth comes from the Sun, but we can also make light with light bulbs and candles. See if you can name three other ways of making light.
