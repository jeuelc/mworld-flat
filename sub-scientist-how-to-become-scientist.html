How to become a scientist?

How do you do science?

You need to begin with a question that you want to answer. You then need to gather evidence, which often involves doing experiments. This experimental evidence is the set of clues that the scientist detective needs to solve the mystery of the question they want to answer!

By studying these clues, the scientist will come to a conclusion as to what the answer is. They then share their answers with the world, by writing about their discoveries in magazines, books, websites and even on Facebook and Twitter!

By sharing their answers with other scientists, everyone has the chance to check the correctness of the answers that the scientists claim as the solution to their problem. This checking is a very important part of the scientific method.
