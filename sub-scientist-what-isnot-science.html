What isn't Science

Scientists make logical findings based on clues. These clues are the evidence scientists use to find the answers to questions that nobody knows the answer to. Scientists can't just make up the answer, they have to be able to tell you the reason that their answer is the right one!

When you answer a question using a hunch or a guess or a feeling that is not based on evidence, that is not science. When you answer a question because you want the answer to be a particular way, that is not science.

To answer a question using science, you have to gather clues (evidence!) and then piece together those clues using a clear and logical argument.
