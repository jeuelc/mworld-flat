Amateur Scientists

 

Most scientists get paid to do science. But there are many important discoveries which have been made by "amateur scientists", which is the name for people who do science for a hobby without being paid. Many important science discoveries have been made by amateur scientists.?

One of the most famous scientists is Albert Einstein, who made his most famous discoveries as an amateur!

Amateur scientists have discovered lots of important things. They have discovered new planets, bubbles in space, how plants inherit features from their parents, and even new types of dinosaur!

What is an orbit?

Does gravity always pull objects together? It does, but this doesn't always result in objects colliding with each other. If objects are set in motion, then they can orbit one another. That means they circle around each other.

A satellite travels so fast horizontally that instead of crashing to Earth, the satellite ends up circling the Earth instead. The Planets in our Solar System have orbited the Sun for billions of years!
