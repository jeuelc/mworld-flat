Why become a scientist?

There are lots of reasons to become a scientist. Some people become scientists because they want to make our lives better. Science has made the world a better place through the invention of the internet, better ways to grow food, and ways to make cars less polluting.

Some become scientists because it is fun. Science is fun because scientists study fascinating unsolved mysteries about how the world works. They enjoy being detectives!

Imagine how much fun it must have been for the scientist who invented the rocket, or the scientist who first thought of the mobile phone, or the scientist who built the first telescope to look at the stars and discovered so many exciting new things about space.If you were a scientist, what questions would you try to answer?
