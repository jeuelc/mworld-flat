How to become a scientist

Scientists are like detectives.

Rather than solving the mystery of a crime, scientists are detectives who find answers to how the world works. They work out many things about the world. They answer questions! There are thousands of questions which scientists can answer.

Why is the sky blue?

What is life?

How does the weather work?

Why does the sun shine?

What are clouds?

Why does the moon hang in the sky?

How do mountains form? How does light work?

Is the earth round?

How does my mobile phone detect my location on a map?

Can you think of some more interesting questions that a scientist could answer?
