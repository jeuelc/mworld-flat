Not sleeping like a baby

 

Whoever came up with the phrase sleep like a baby never had a baby in their house!

Babies sleep in short bursts, waking every few hours at all times of the day and night to eat and look around and bawl! before going back to sleep again. For anyone who is not a baby, this would be a terrible way to sleep.

So how do you make sure you dont sleep like a baby? The answer is simple be nice to your body clock and have a routine! Try to go to bed at the same time every night. Turn off all your electronic devices at least 30 minutes before you want to go to sleep. Dont drink anything with caffeine in it at least two hours before you plan to go to bed. Wind down by reading a book or listening to soft music.
