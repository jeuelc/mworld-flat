Remembering your dreams

 

Why is it that sometimes you remember your dreams and sometimes you dont? It all has to do with when you wake up.

Most of your REM sleep occurs early in the morning just before you wake up. If you wake up when you are in REM sleep, you will remember what you have just been dreaming. But not for long! It is only stored in your short-term memory. And when we say a very short time, we mean a very short time only ten seconds or less! So if you have a cool dream that seems like a big adventure, it might have only been a five second dream!

If you want to remember your dreams for longer, you need to talk about them or write them down. Then they will be stored in your longer-term memory.
