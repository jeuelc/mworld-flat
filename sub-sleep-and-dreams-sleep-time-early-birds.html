Early birds versus night owls

 

Is it better to be an early bird or a night owl? Well, neither is better than the other. They are just different. Being an early bird or a night owl has to do with the timing of your body clock.

If you are an early bird, your body clock will start telling you it's time for sleep earlier in the night than would be normally expected. If you are a night owl, you will get the sleep signals much later than would be expected. Scientists call this a circadian shift. This also means that early birds are at their best in the morning or early afternoon, whereas the night owls don't usually get going until late morning but still have a lot of energy at night.
