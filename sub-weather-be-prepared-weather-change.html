A change in the weather

 
click here

Our atmosphere is changing. The fuel that people burn to make electricity releases gases, called greenhouse gases, into the atmosphere. These gases act like an extra blanket over the earth, causing the atmosphere to heat up. This is known as the greenhouse effect.

The greenhouse effect has caused global warming, heating the earth by almost 1°C since the year 1900. Another way of saying global warming is 'climate change' because there are also changes to the climate, including extreme weather.

In recent years there have been more heatwaves around the world. Some places are getting more droughts, while others are getting more floods. Scientists are confident that some of these changes are because of climate change, and they say these extremes will probably change more in the future.

So let's all put our thinking caps on, because together we can work out how to best handle extreme weather in a warming world!
