Be Prepared!

Extreme weather can be very hot, very cold, very windy, very rainy, very dusty, very snowy or very loud! Most of the time, it's amazing to watch and experience, but in some cases it can also be downright dangerous and scary.

Extreme weather can cause damage to our communities, homes and sometimes to us if we are not careful.

As people who live with extreme weather know, the best way to stay safe is to be prepared!
