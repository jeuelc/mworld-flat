Wacky weather

 
click here click here

The weather always changes and sometimes, it can get a little wacky. Instead of being a nice warm day to go to the park, it can be too hot to even be outside! And instead of light morning rain, it's like someone has tipped a bucket of water from the sky!

It's this type of weather that goes beyond what we usually experience every day. This is weather to the extreme!

When it rolls in, extreme weather can do things that normal, everyday weather doesn't do. Instead of rain, balls of ice, called hail, can fall from the sky. Storm clouds can flash and crash with lightning and thunder and make wild, whirling winds. Wacky weather never lasts for long, but when it arrives, it can make a spectacular sound and light show. Just make sure you're inside to watch!

What is an orbit?

Does gravity always pull objects together? It does, but this doesn't always result in objects colliding with each other. If objects are set in motion, then they can orbit one another. That means they circle around each other.

A satellite travels so fast horizontally that instead of crashing to Earth, the satellite ends up circling the Earth instead. The Planets in our Solar System have orbited the Sun for billions of years!
