Soaked and squally

There is one type of weather system that is more extreme than any other. One that can bring soaking rains so heavy that streets end up looking like rivers, and squally winds so strong that even some buildings can topple over in their wake. We're talking about storms!

There isn't just one type of storm, there are a family of them and each is a little different. From the small, baby thunderstorms, which might give just a single flash of lightning and a rumble of thunder, right through to the big daddy storm, the tropical cyclone. Each storm is created in a unique way and has its own personality.

Come and see what happens inside them!
