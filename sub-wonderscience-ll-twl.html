Tricks with light

Light can play tricks on us! When we look at ourselves in a mirror that has a wavy surface, we look funny! When we look at the surface of the road on a hot day, it seems to shimmer like the surface of water on a sunny day! Sometimes, in the desert, this heat shimmer above the surface of hot sand can look like there is water present when there is nothing but sand! This is called a "mirage".

There are many other tricks of light too.
